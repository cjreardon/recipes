Pasta Recipe

Description

This is actually the easiest thing you could possibly make. Don't mess it up.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • black pepper
  • salt

Preparation Steps

  1. In a medium sauce pan, boil enough water to cover your pasta.

  2. Add the pasta top the boiling water

  3. Wait 8-10 minutes for pasta to cook.

  4. Drain the pasta in a collinder, over the sink.

  5. Add butter, salt, and pepper. Use as much or as little as you want.

  6. Mix

  7. Transfer to serving plates.
